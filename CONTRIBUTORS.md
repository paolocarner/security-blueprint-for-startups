## Acknowledgments

### Framework Development
This security blueprint was developed based on extensive research and industry best practices, incorporating guidance from leading cybersecurity organizations and frameworks including:

- Center for Internet Security (CIS) Controls
- National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST) Cybersecurity Framework
- International Organization for Standardization (ISO 27001)
- OWASP Security Guidelines
- SANS Institute Security Resources

### Industry Expertise
The framework incorporates insights and lessons learned from cybersecurity professionals working with technology startups across various industries and geographic regions. We acknowledge the valuable contributions of the broader cybersecurity community in developing practical, effective security guidance for startup environments.

### Community Feedback
The blueprint has been refined based on feedback from startup executives, security professionals, and technology practitioners who have implemented security programs in resource-constrained environments. Their real-world experiences and insights have been invaluable in creating practical, actionable guidance.

## How to Become a Contributor

We welcome contributions from individuals and organizations who share our commitment to improving cybersecurity in technology startup environments. Contributors can participate through:

- Documentation improvements and corrections
- Framework enhancements and additions
- Industry-specific adaptations and guidance
- Tool evaluations and recommendations
- Implementation case studies and examples
- Translation and localization efforts

See our [Contributing Guidelines](CONTRIBUTING.md) for detailed information on how to contribute to the project.
