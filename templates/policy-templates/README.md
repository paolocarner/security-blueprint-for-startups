# Security Policy Templates

This directory contains comprehensive security policy templates designed specifically for technology startups implementing the Tech Startup Security Blueprint. These templates provide practical, legally-reviewed policy frameworks that can be customized to meet specific organizational needs and regulatory requirements.

## Available Policy Templates

### Core Security Policies
- **Information Security Policy**: Overarching security governance framework
- **Acceptable Use Policy**: Guidelines for technology resource usage
- **Data Classification Policy**: Framework for data handling and protection
- **Access Control Policy**: User access management and authorization procedures
- **Incident Response Policy**: Security incident handling and escalation procedures

### Compliance-Specific Policies
- **Privacy Policy**: Data privacy and protection requirements (GDPR/CCPA compliant)
- **Data Retention Policy**: Information lifecycle management procedures
- **Vendor Management Policy**: Third-party security assessment and management
- **Business Continuity Policy**: Disaster recovery and business continuity planning
- **Change Management Policy**: Secure change control procedures

### Technical Security Policies
- **Network Security Policy**: Network architecture and security controls
- **Endpoint Security Policy**: Device management and protection requirements
- **Cloud Security Policy**: Cloud service usage and security requirements
- **Application Security Policy**: Secure development and deployment practices
- **Backup and Recovery Policy**: Data backup and recovery procedures

## Template Customization Guide

Each policy template includes:
- **Placeholder sections** for organization-specific information
- **Regulatory compliance mappings** for common frameworks
- **Implementation guidance** and best practices
- **Review and approval workflows** for policy management
- **Training and awareness requirements** for policy communication

## Usage Instructions

1. **Select appropriate templates** based on your regulatory requirements and business needs
2. **Customize placeholder content** with organization-specific information and requirements
3. **Review with legal counsel** to ensure compliance with applicable laws and regulations
4. **Obtain executive approval** through established governance processes
5. **Implement training programs** to ensure employee awareness and compliance
6. **Establish review cycles** for ongoing policy maintenance and updates

## Compliance Mapping

Templates include specific guidance for:
- **SOC 2 Type II** compliance requirements
- **ISO 27001** information security management
- **GDPR** data protection and privacy requirements
- **HIPAA** healthcare information protection (where applicable)
- **PCI DSS** payment card data security (where applicable)

*Note: These templates provide starting points for policy development and should be reviewed by qualified legal counsel before implementation to ensure compliance with applicable laws and regulations in your jurisdiction.*
